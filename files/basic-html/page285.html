WOUND CARE ACCESSORIES
Cardinal HealthTM Essentials Silicone Non-Adhesive Foam and Silicone Adhesive Border Foam Dressings*
Sterile wound dressing that consists of hydrophilic, absorbent, polyurethane foam that is designed to absorb exudate. The outer layer of the dressing is a pink, low friction, waterproof, polyurethane  lm that provides a bacterial barrier. The perforated, silicone adhesive, wound contact layer enables exudate uptake and prevents excess  uid that can
cause maceration to healthy skin. Perforated wound contact layer prevents the dressing from sticking to the wound and reduces pain during dressing changes, by preventing the formation of granulation tissue into the pores of the foam. Maintains a moist environment to assist in the healing process of moderate to heavily exuding wounds. Provides a gentle but secure adhesion to skin.
