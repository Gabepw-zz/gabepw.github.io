OmniPod® Personal Diabetes Manager Starter Kit
Tubing-free OmniPod® System simpli es pump therapy with only two parts that integrate the reservoir, infusion set and inserter. Designed to be worn almost anywhere on the body, the small, waterproof Pod delivers up to 200 units of U-100 rapid acting
